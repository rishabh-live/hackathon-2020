Website Visitors

Like most website operators, SMITSONIAN collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. SMITSONIAN only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below.

Cookies

To enrich and perfect your online experience, SMITSONIAN uses "Cookies", similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. SMITSONIAN uses cookies to help SMITSONIAN identify and track visitors, their usage of https://www.SMITSONIAN.com, and their website access preferences. SMITSONIAN visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using SMITSONIAN's websites, with the drawback that certain features of SMITSONIAN's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to SMITSONIAN's use of cookies.
